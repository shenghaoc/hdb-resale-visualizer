# Product Purpose

## Vision
A map-first Singapore HDB resale explorer built for making real buying decisions, not for price prediction. The product aims to provide transparent, deterministic, and high-signal data to prospective HDB buyers.

## Target User
Prospective HDB buyers in Singapore who are eligible for resale flats. They value efficiency, accuracy, and clear visual context (maps) over complex predictive models.

## Key Principles
1. **Deterministic Over Predictive**: Show historical facts and proximity data. Never invent scores or forecasts.
2. **Map-First**: Location is the primary filter. Use the map to provide grounding for all decision factors.
3. **High Information Density**: Provide expert-level data (PSF, market percentiles, school counts) in a concise, accessible interface.
4. **Privacy & Trust**: Strictly browser-local state for user preferences. Zero tracking or server-side storage of user shortlists.
